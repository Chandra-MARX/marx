Bert, Dan, Ron: The following file contains more detailed encircled
energy and other mirror information. Hope that it's useful.

Leon Van Speybroeck





This file consists of four parts.

The first part gives the geometric definition of the
current mirror design (as of Feb. 1, 1993). The "uninteresting"
data for pairs 2 and 5 are included.

The second part gives the effective areas.

The third part gives predicted encircled energy performance
for our current guesses at P1H1 properties and assuming that
the remaining elements meet their specifications.

The fourth part gives predicted encircled energy performance
for our current guesses at P1H1 properties and assuming that
the remaining elements meet their goals.

There are important (similar) notes at the beginnings of parts 3 and 4.
The program inputs used for these calculations also are included.
